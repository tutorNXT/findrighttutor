The Origin Story

"3 Boys from Kharagpur Started This."

What happens when three visionary engineering minds cross paths in the prestigious academic and industrial hub of 'Kharagpur' ? They decide to systematically redefine rural and local academic discovery.

Frustrated by how difficult it was for students in local neighborhoods to access verified study support, Sahil, Amrit and Shaswat engineered 'TutorNxt'. This isn't just a directory—it is a centralized learning bridge built with extreme technical passion and academic discipline.
